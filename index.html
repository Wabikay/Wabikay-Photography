About Wabikay

Where Light Meets Narrative

Wabikay Photography is a Lagos-based visual storytelling brand dedicated to capturing authentic moments, raw emotions, and cherished memories through the art of timeless photography.

Every assignment is approached with intention — from the golden hour of a wedding morning to the quiet intimacy of a documentary portrait. We don't just take photographs; we preserve the stories your children's children will one day hold.

With roots in Nigeria and a vision that transcends borders, Wabikay has become a trusted name for clients who value artistry, authenticity, and legacy.
